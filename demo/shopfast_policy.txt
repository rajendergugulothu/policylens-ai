SHOPFAST RETURN & REFUND POLICY — v4.2 (Effective June 2026)

Section 1 — Standard Returns
Customers may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full cash refund.
Items must be unused, in original packaging, with all accessories included.
The 30-day window begins at the delivery date, not the order date.

Section 2 — Final Sale Items
Items marked "Final Sale" at checkout are non-returnable and non-refundable.
This includes all items in the ShopFast Clearance Store priced below $5.00,
all trading card products, and limited-edition drops.
Exception: Final Sale items that arrive damaged, defective, or materially different
from the listing qualify for STORE CREDIT ONLY (not cash refund).
Customer must provide photo evidence within 5 days of delivery.

Section 3 — Non-Returnable Categories
The following may not be returned under any circumstances:
digital downloads and software licenses once activated; perishable goods;
customized or personalized items; intimate apparel and swimwear (hygiene).
If any item in these categories arrives damaged, the Section 5 exception applies.

Section 4 — Marketplace Seller Items
Items sold by ShopFast Marketplace partners are governed by the individual
seller's return policy. ShopFast agents must NOT approve direct refunds on
marketplace items. The agent must route the return request to the seller first.
ShopFast Guarantee applies if: (a) seller does not respond within 48 hours,
or (b) item arrives materially different from the listing.

Section 5 — Damaged or Defective Items
Any item (including Final Sale and non-returnable) that arrives damaged,
defective, or materially different qualifies for:
- Standard items: full CASH REFUND or replacement (customer's choice)
- Final Sale items: STORE CREDIT ONLY (not cash refund)
Customer must contact support within 7 days with photo evidence.

Section 6 — Holiday Extension
Items purchased between November 1 and December 31 may be returned
until January 31 of the following year.
EXCEPTION: Apple-branded products purchased in this window must be
returned by January 15 (not January 31).
ShopFast Gift Cards are excluded from the holiday extension entirely.

Section 7 — Loyalty Points Purchases
When a purchase was made using ShopFast Loyalty Points combined with cash:
- The cash portion of the refund is issued as a CASH REFUND to the original payment method
- The loyalty points portion of the refund is issued as STORE CREDIT
These are processed separately. The agent must split the refund proportionally.
Agents must NOT issue the entire refund as cash when loyalty points were used.
